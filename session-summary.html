Key Research Insights Applied

90/10 Rule

90% prevention, 10% instruction - Focus on what NOT to do prevents more issues than explaining what to do.

Decision Trees

Structured logic for ambiguity - Clear paths for handling different types of queries and information.

Binary Rules

Hard on/off switches - No fuzzy language like "try to" or "maybe" - just clear yes/no rules.
